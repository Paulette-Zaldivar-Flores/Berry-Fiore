My Story

Hi my name is Yesenia and I am the owner of Berry Fiore. I founded Berry Fiore in November 2020 when the pandemic hit. I am a stay at home mom who struggled with unemployment during the pandemic. I wanted to continue contributing to my house hold. With the help of my husband and loved ones we were able to establish Berry Fiore and now have over 1,500 followers in Instagram and regular clients.

My passion for dipping strawberries began I would offer to help my in-laws with making traditional Valentines Day desserts for our yearly Valentines Day dinner. As I served and observed my in-laws enjoy the desserts I helped make that I would love to spread this joy to others. So I decided to make it a career by first sharing my desserts in small community events. This helped spread the joy of Berry Fiore all over the South San Diego Community.
